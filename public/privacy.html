Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Spark, we collect the following information:

  • Account data: username, email address, and a bcrypt-hashed password
  • Profile data: display name, age, gender, bio, and profile photo (if you choose to provide them)
  • Messages: chat content, file attachments, and timestamps sent through the app
  • Last-seen status: timestamp of your last activity for presence indicators

2. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally in a JSON file (db.json) on the server where the app is running. We do not use external databases or cloud storage services. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using bcrypt before storage.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core functionality: user accounts, profiles, messaging, and online presence
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns
  • To respond to contact form inquiries sent through the app
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Spark uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token (JWT) so you stay logged in across page refreshes. This token expires after 7 days. No cookies are used for tracking or advertising purposes.

5. Data You Choose to Share

Profile information (name, age, gender, bio, photo) is visible to other registered users of the app. Messages are visible only to the sender and recipient. Do not share sensitive personal information you wouldn't want others to see.

6. Data Deletion

You can request account deletion by contacting the administrator through the Admin panel. When an account is deleted, all associated data (profile, messages, photos) are permanently removed from the database.

7. Third-Party Services

The Contact form on the Profile page uses Formspree to relay messages to the developer's email. Formspree's privacy policy applies to those submissions. No other third-party services have access to your data.

8. Security

We implement basic security measures including password hashing (bcrypt), JWT authentication tokens, and admin-only access to sensitive endpoints. However, this app is intended for development and educational use — please do not store highly sensitive information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out via the Contact form on the Profile page or email the administrator.
