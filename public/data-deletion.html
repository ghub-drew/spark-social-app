Data Deletion

Last updated: May 8, 2026

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their personal data associated with Spark.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to the administrator with the email address you used to register.

Email your request to:
siomai2025@gmail.com

Subject:
Account Deletion Request — Spark

Include in your email:
Your registered email address or username

What Gets Deleted

Once your request is verified, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account credentials (username, email, hashed password)
  • Profile information (display name, age, gender, bio)
  • Profile photo and uploaded files
  • Messages and conversation history
  • Account activity records

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data Retained

No personal data is retained after account deletion. For full details, refer to our Privacy Policy.

Contact

For questions about data deletion, reach out via the contact form on the Profile page or email the administrator.
